Competition Rules

To ensure fair judging and encourage authentic creativity, please follow these guidelines.

Kid-Created Only

All work must be created by the child without AI assistance or significant adult help.

Monthly Deadline

Submissions are due by the last day of each month. New challenges are announced on the 1st.

Original Work

All submissions must be original creations, not copied from existing sources.

Email Submission

Send your creation directly to our competition email with a brief description.

How to Submit Your Creation

Follow these simple steps to share your creative solution.

1

Create Your Project

Work on your challenge entry following the monthly theme. Remember - no AI or significant adult help!

2

Prepare Your Submission

Write a brief description of your creation and what problem it solves.

3

Email Your Creation

Send your submission to our competition email with the following information:

  • Your name and age
  • Description of your project
  • Any images you'd like to share (optional)

Wait for Results
